How to Use the Secondary Dashboard

Welcome! This guide will help you explore and make the most of all features available in the Secondary Dashboard. Enjoy the journey!

  • Get a quick overview of total students, teachers, and parents.
  • See recent activities and updates at a glance.
  • Use this as your starting point to navigate the system.

  • View all upcoming and past school events.
  • Admins can add, edit, or delete events to keep everyone informed.
  • Stay updated on important dates like meetings, exams, and holidays.

  • View all classes (Form 1-6) and their streams.
  • Add, edit, or remove forms and assign class teachers.
  • Manage subjects for each form.

  • Enter marks for students by form and subject.
  • Review and update marks as needed.
  • Automatic grade calculation and validation.

  • Update school details, logo, and contact information.
  • Manage subjects, grades, and divisions for assessments.
  • Customize the system to fit your school's needs.

  • Generate detailed performance reports for students.
  • View, print, or export reports for each class or student.
  • Track progress and identify areas for improvement.

  • Keep your account secure by regularly updating your password.
  • Follow password best practices for safety.

  • Send important announcements to parents and staff.
  • Receive updates and notifications from the school.
  • Keep communication organized and accessible.

  • View a list of all secondary school teachers.
  • Manage teacher assignments and details.
  • Promote collaboration among staff.

  • Upload and view timetables for each form (Form 1-6).
  • Ensure students and teachers are aware of class schedules.
  • Download timetables for offline use.

  • Access a wide range of past exam papers from trusted sources.
  • Use past papers for revision, practice, and exam preparation.
  • Links to Maktaba, Waza Elimu, Learning Hub Tanzania, Different Schools, and more.

Tip: Click any icon on the dashboard to explore its features. The system is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. Enjoy discovering all the tools at your fingertips!
